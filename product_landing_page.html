Who

Anyone invited to 54Lunc.com may use it to organize lunches.

What

The 54Lunch.com website is powered by FireBase.com (from Google). As long as the volume stays within the free tier, everything will be great.

When

You can oranize lunches anytime you want. Or set your group to have lunches on a schedule (e.g. monthly).
